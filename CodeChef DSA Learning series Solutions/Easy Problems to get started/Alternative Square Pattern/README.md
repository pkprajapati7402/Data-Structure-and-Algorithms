Alternative Square Pattern Problem Code: SQALPAT
Add problem to Todo list

You're given a number N. Print the first N lines of the below-given pattern.

1 2 3 4 5
10 9 8 7 6
11 12 13 14 15
20 19 18 17 16
21 22 23 24 25
30 29 28 27 26
Input:
First-line will contain the number N.
Output:
Print the first N lines of the given pattern.

Constraints
1≤N≤200
Sample Input 1:
4
Sample Output 1:
1 2 3 4 5
10 9 8 7 6
11 12 13 14 15
20 19 18 17 16
Sample Input 2:
2
Sample Output 2:
1 2 3 4 5
10 9 8 7 6
EXPLANATION:
In the first example, we'll print the first 4 lines of the given pattern.
In the second example, we'll print the first 2 lines of the given pattern.
